Family Manager Account

Your Family Manager Account includes all the features of a Primary Account, providing you with an all-inclusive access to the CozyCircles community.

In addition to Primary Account features, as a Family Manager, you have the special ability to monitor a linked Primary Account. This is ideal for family members, children, or others who need extra care and monitoring.

  • Connections Monitoring:
  • Keep track of connections your linked Primary Account makes with other users.
  • Circles Monitoring:
  • Monitor the Circles your linked Primary Account is included in or creates.
  • Instant Messaging Monitoring:
  • View the Instant Messaging activity of your linked Primary Account.

Your Family Manager role ensures a secure and supportive environment for your loved ones, including those who may require additional attention. Connect, share, and engage while providing the necessary care and supervision.

Caregiver Account

As a Caregiver on CozyCircles, you play a crucial role in supporting and connecting with users facing various challenges.
Your account is tailored to provide assistance and resources, fostering a caring community.
Caregiver accounts are designed for individuals who serve as caregivers outside of CozyCircles, as well as professionals working in fields related to caring for others.

  • Connection Support:
  • Assist users in connecting with others who share similar experiences.
  • Resource Sharing:
  • Share valuable resources, tips, and information to aid in everyday challenges.
  • Support Groups:
  • Create or join support groups to connect with other caregivers and share insights.
  • Direct Assistance:
  • Offer direct assistance and support to individuals who may need personalized help.
  • Tailored Circles:
  • Create private circles for specific caregiving purposes or join circles that align with your interests.
  • Community Building:
  • Contribute to building a compassionate community by sharing experiences and advice.
