Community Guidelines

Welcome to CozyCircles, a supportive and inclusive space for individuals of all ages. To ensure a positive and respectful environment, please adhere to the following community guidelines:

Be Kind and Respectful

Treat others with kindness, empathy, and respect. This involves actively listening to different perspectives, avoiding offensive language or tone, and refraining from personal attacks. Engage in constructive and positive interactions, fostering an environment where everyone feels valued and understood.

No Bullying or Harassment

Bullying and harassment are strictly prohibited. Bullying includes any repeated, harmful actions intended to intimidate, humiliate, or harm others. Harassment includes any unwanted, persistent, or offensive behavior that creates an uncomfortable environment for other users. If you witness or experience bullying or harassment, promptly report it for thorough investigation and resolution.

Maintain Privacy

Respecting privacy is vital to creating a safe community. Do not share personal information, such as addresses, phone numbers, or financial details, without explicit consent. Exercise caution when sharing your own personal information. If you suspect any breaches of privacy, promptly report them to ensure a swift resolution.

No Offensive Content

Ensure that all content, including text, images, videos, and links, adheres to our standards of appropriateness. Offensive content includes but is not limited to hate speech, discriminatory language, violence, sexually explicit material, or any content that may cause discomfort or distress. Help us cultivate a positive and safe environment by reporting any offensive content you encounter.

Stay on Topic

Contribute to discussions that align with the purpose and themes of CozyCircles. Avoid spamming, posting irrelevant content, or derailing discussions. If you have a new topic or idea, consider starting a new thread or group to maintain the clarity and organization of conversations.

Report Violations

Our reporting system is designed to empower the community to maintain a healthy environment. If you come across any content or behavior that violates our guidelines, use the reporting feature to alert our moderation team. We take every report seriously and will conduct a thorough review to ensure appropriate action is taken.

No Impersonation

Impersonating other users, public figures, or creating fake accounts undermines the trust within our community. Be genuine and transparent in your interactions. If you suspect someone is impersonating another user, report it immediately.

Child Safety

CozyCircles is committed to being a family-friendly platform. Refrain from posting or sharing content that could be deemed inappropriate for users of all ages. Report any content that raises concerns about child safety promptly. Our goal is to provide a secure environment for users of every age group.

Compliance with Laws

Abide by all applicable laws and regulations in your local jurisdiction. Do not engage in or promote any illegal activities on the platform, as this will result in immediate account suspension.

Consequences for Violations

Violations of these guidelines may result in consequences such as content removal, account suspension, or banning. The severity of consequences will be determined based on the nature and recurrence of the violation. Our priority is to maintain a safe, enjoyable, and respectful community for all users.

Feedback and Suggestions

We value open communication and encourage users to provide constructive feedback or suggestions for improving the community. Your input is crucial to enhancing the overall user experience, and we appreciate your commitment to making CozyCircles a better place.

Any violation of the community guidelines may result in immediate account suspension or ban to ensure a safe and respectful environment for all users.
