Welcome to our FAQ's Page

Below you'll find answers to our most Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

  • What is CozyCircles?

    CozyCircles is an online community designed to connect and support individuals with disabilities, their families, and caregivers. It offers a warm and inclusive space for sharing experiences and fostering connections.

  • Who can join CozyCircles?

    CozyCircles is open to everyone, with a primary focus on individuals with disabilities, their families, and caregivers. We welcome diversity and encourage anyone to join our community.

Account Related

  • How do i create an account on CozyCircles?

    To create an account, click on the "Create Account" button, follow the registration process, and provide the required information.

  • Can I link multiple account types to one profile?

    At the moment, each user account is associated with a specific account type. If you have different roles, consider creating separate accounts.

Privacy and Security

  • How is my privacy protected on CozyCircles?

    CozyCircles takes user privacy seriously. We have robust security measures in place to protect your data. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for detailed information.

  • Is my information visible to everyone on the platform?

    No, CozyCircles provides privacy controls allowing you to manage who can see your information. You can customize your privacy settings in your account.

Community Guidelines

  • What are the community guidelines?

    Our community guidelines outline the expected behavior on CozyCircles. We encourage all users to review and adhere to these guidelines to maintain a positive and supportive environment

Troubleshooting

  • I'm experiencing issues with my account. What should I do?

    If you encounter any problems, please reach out to our Support Team for assistance. Provide detailed information about the issue for a prompt resolution.
